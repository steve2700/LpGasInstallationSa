## Gas Braai Safety & Maintenance (300 words)

### Safety Features:

**Flame Failure Device:**
Automatically cuts gas if flames extinguish (wind, spills).

**Individual Burner Controls:**
Control heat zones independently and shut off unused burners.

**Drip Pan System:**
Catches grease safely away from flames.

**Hood and Ventilation:**
Proper airflow prevents gas accumulation.

### Safe Operation:

**DO:**
- Check for gas leaks before each use (soap test)
- Keep braai area clear of combustibles
- Never leave operating braai unattended
- Use appropriate gas bottles/supply
- Clean regularly to prevent grease fires
- Store gas bottles properly when not in use
- Shut off gas at bottle after use

**DON'T:**
- Use braai indoors or in enclosed spaces
- Store gas bottles in enclosed areas
- Exceed recommended gas pressure
- Use damaged or worn hoses
- Allow children to operate unattended
- Use water on gas fires (use extinguisher)

### Maintenance Schedule:

**After Each Use:**
- Clean grill grates while warm
- Empty drip pans
- Wipe down exterior

**Monthly (for frequent users):**
- Deep clean burners
- Check gas connections
- Inspect hoses for damage
- Clean venturi tubes

**Annually:**
- Professional service inspection
- Replace worn parts (igniters, thermocouples)
- Check gas pressure
- Burner cleaning and adjustment

[Link to: /gas-maintenance-services-gauteng]

### Common Issues & Solutions:

**Uneven Heating:**
- Clean burners and venturi tubes
- Check for blocked ports

**Ignition Problems:**
- Clean ignition electrodes
- Replace batteries (electronic ignition)
- Check electrode gap

**Yellow Flames:**
- Clean burners (should be blue)
- Check air shutter adjustment
- Verify proper gas pressure

## Frequently Asked Questions (300 words)

### What size gas braai do I need?
Depends on entertaining frequency and guest numbers:
- **2-3 burners:** 4-8 people regularly
- **4 burners:** 8-12 people regularly
- **6 burners:** 12+ people or frequent large gatherings

### Can I convert my charcoal braai to gas?
Sometimes. Built-in masonry braais can sometimes accommodate gas braai heads, but modification may be needed. We assess each situation individually.

### How much gas does a braai use?
Average 3-hour braai session uses 0.8-1.2kg LPG. A 9kg bottle provides 8-12 braais.

### Where should gas bottles be stored?
Outdoors, in well-ventilated area, away from ignition sources. Never indoors, in garages, or enclosed spaces.

### Can I use my gas braai in the rain?
Yes, quality gas braais are weatherproof. However, cover when not in use to extend lifespan.

### How long do gas braais last?
Quality stainless steel gas braais last 10-15 years with proper maintenance. Cheaper models may last 5-7 years.

### Do I need gas COC for a braai?
Yes, all permanent gas installations including braais require Certificate of Compliance.

### Can I DIY install a gas braai?
No. Gas installations must be performed by qualified SAQCC Gas registered installers. DIY is illegal and dangerous.

### What's better: natural gas or LPG?
Both work excellently. LPG (bottled gas) is more common as most homes don't have piped natural gas. LPG actually burns hotter.

### Can I add a rotisserie to my gas braai?
Many gas braais have optional rotisserie kits available. Some need electrical connection for motor.

### How far from the house should a gas braai be?
Minimum 1m from combustible structures. More distance is better for safety and smoke management.
