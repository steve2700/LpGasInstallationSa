## Gas Fireplace Safety & Maintenance (300 words)

### Safety Features in Modern Gas Fireplaces:

**Oxygen Depletion Sensor (ODS):**
Automatically shuts off gas if oxygen levels drop too low (essential for flueless models).

**Flame Failure Device:**
Cuts gas supply if flame extinguishes unexpectedly.

**Cool-Touch Glass:**
Special glass stays cooler to prevent burns (especially important for homes with children).

**Safety Barrier:**
Screen or guard keeps children and pets away from flames and hot surfaces.

**Thermostat Control:**
Prevents overheating by cycling fireplace on/off to maintain set temperature.

### Safe Operation Guidelines:

**DO:**
- Keep flue/chimney clear and unobstructed
- Ensure adequate room ventilation
- Keep combustible materials away (furniture, curtains)
- Use fireplace screen if provided
- Supervise children and pets
- Turn off when leaving home or sleeping

**DON'T:**
- Block air vents or intakes
- Use fireplace for drying clothes
- Spray aerosols near flames
- Touch hot glass or surfaces
- Modify fireplace settings beyond manufacturer specs

### Annual Maintenance Requirements:

**Professional Service Includes:**
- Clean burner and pilot assembly
- Check and clean flue/chimney
- Inspect gas connections for leaks
- Test safety devices (ODS, FFD)
- Clean glass and logs/media
- Verify proper combustion (blue flames)
- Check for carbon monoxide
- Lubricate moving parts
- Test remote/thermostat operation

**Cost:** R500-R800 annually

## Frequently Asked Questions (300 words)

### How much does it cost to run a gas fireplace?
A typical gas fireplace costs R4-R6 per hour to operate, or R400-R600 monthly if used 4 hours daily. Much cheaper than electric heating.

### Do gas fireplaces need a chimney?
Most gas fireplaces need a flue (chimney) to expel combustion gases. Flueless models exist but have limitations and strict ventilation requirements.

### Can a gas fireplace heat my whole house?
Gas fireplaces are designed for zone heating (one room or open area). They're not central heating systems, but they effectively heat living areas where you spend most time.

### Do gas fireplaces work during loadshedding?
Most gas fireplaces work without electricity. Models with electronic ignition can usually be lit manually with matches during power outages.

### How long do gas fireplaces last?
Quality gas fireplaces last 15-20 years with proper maintenance. Burners and control systems are serviceable and replaceable.

### Are gas fireplaces safe?
Yes, when professionally installed and properly maintained. Modern gas fireplaces have multiple safety features and produce negligible indoor emissions when correctly installed.

### Can I install a gas fireplace in a bedroom?
Yes, but with important considerations:
- Adequate ventilation required
- Room size matters (minimum requirements)
- Safety features essential
- Professional assessment recommended

### What's the difference between vented and flueless?
Vented (flued) fireplaces expel combustion gases outside via chimney/flue. Flueless fireplaces use catalytic converters and require excellent room ventilation. Vented is generally preferred for safety.

### Do I need a gas COC for a fireplace?
Yes, Certificate of Compliance is legally required for all permanent gas installations including fireplaces.

### Can I DIY install a gas fireplace?
Absolutely not. Gas fireplace installation requires qualified SAQCC Gas registered installers. DIY installation is illegal and extremely dangerous.
